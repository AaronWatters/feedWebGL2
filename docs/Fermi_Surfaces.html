Fermi Surfaces

This interactive visualization explores theoretical electron energy levels in a crystal lattice.

The surfaces displayed approximates where the electron energy reaches an adjustable cut off value.

It uses WebGL2 features which are not supported on all browser configurations.

wire frame rotator

Interactive Controls

Rotate all figures by dragging the mouse over the central wireframe.

Adjust the sliders to change the energy cutoff value for any figure on the top or bottom.

Check or uncheck the check box for any figure on the top or bottom to add or remove it from the wireframe.
